About Vibiz

Our Mission

Make professional marketing accessible to every business owner, regardless of technical skills or marketing experience.

The Problem We Solve

Starting a business is hard enough. But today, entrepreneurs need to master a dozen different tools just to get their first customer. Landing pages, email marketing, social media ads, analytics — it's overwhelming and expensive.

We watched talented business owners spend months learning marketing instead of focusing on what they do best. We saw great products fail because their creators couldn't afford $5,000/month agencies. That's when we realized: AI could change everything.

Why We Built Vibiz

In 2023, we asked ourselves: What if AI could run your entire marketing department? Not just generate copy or images, but actually make strategic decisions, launch campaigns, and optimize for conversions — all automatically.

After 18 months of development and testing with over 5,000 beta users, Vibiz was born. It's the first AI platform that doesn't just help you build marketing assets — it actually runs your marketing, 24/7.

How We're Different

While other tools focus on templates and drag-and-drop editors, we focus on results. Vibiz doesn't ask you to choose designs or write headlines. Instead, it learns about your business and creates everything from scratch, optimized for your specific market.

Our AI analyzes millions of successful campaigns to understand what works. It tests variations faster than any human team could. And it never stops improving your results.

Our Values

  • Simplicity First: If it takes more than 5 minutes, we've failed.
  • Results Over Features: We measure success by your revenue, not our feature list.
  • Accessible to All: Professional marketing shouldn't require professional marketers.
  • Continuous Innovation: Our AI learns from every campaign to get better for everyone.

The Team

Vibiz was founded by a team of AI researchers, marketing veterans, and serial entrepreneurs who believed there had to be a better way. We're backed by leading investors in AI and marketing technology.
